## Performance Overview
* Total Revenue Generated: $2.3M with $286K in Profit.
* Overall Profit Margin: 12.5% (strong potential for optimization).
* High Discount Impact: Products with 30%+ discount saw a sharp drop in profitability
* High Geographic Concentration: 99% of revenue comes from just 3 states (CA, NY, TX), 
California leads with $741,999 in sales (32% of total revenue)
New York: $719,047 (31% of total revenue)
Texas: $836,154 (36% of total revenue)
* Strong Seasonal Peaks: Q4 generates 30%+ of annual revenue.
* Strong Sales Volume: Office Supplies and Technology led in total revenue.

## Customer And Segment Performance
* Top Customer Segment: Corporate customers generated the highest profit per order.
* Regional Trends: The West region outperformed others in both sales and margin.
* Category Strength: Technology accessories delivered high profit with low returns.
* Weak Spot: Furniture category struggled with negative profit despite strong demand.

* ## Sales & Operational Trends
* Order Priority: Standard shipping dominated, but Same-Day orders had the lowest profitability.
* High-Risk Items: Several sub-categories in Furniture consistently incurred losses.
* Seasonality: Strong Q4 spike driven by corporate bulk purchases.

## Recommendations
* Immediate Action: Reduce aggressive discounting on high-demand items, especially in Furniture, to prevent avoidable losses.
* Profit Optimization: Bundle low-margin items with profitable accessories to stabilize margins.
* Customer Strategy: Double down on Corporate and West region customers they deliver the most reliable ROI.
* Operational Improvements: Evaluate shipping strategies to limit cost leakage on low-value orders.
* Category Focus: Scale Technology and Office Supplies, while restructuring pricing for underperforming Furniture groups.
